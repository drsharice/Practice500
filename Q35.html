Register an application with App Registration

For the most secure operation, register your app with the Microsoft identity platform.

Before your app can get a token from the Microsoft identity platform, it must be registered in the Azure portal. Registration integrates your app with the Microsoft identity platform and establishes the information that it uses to get tokens, including:


A unique identifier assigned by the Microsoft identity platform.


One or more endpoints at which your app will receive responses from the Microsoft identity platform. (For native and mobile apps, this is a URI assigned by the Microsoft identity platform.)


A password or a public/private key pair that your app uses to authenticate with the Microsoft identity platform. (Not needed for native or mobile apps.)
